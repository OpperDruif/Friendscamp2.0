Festival Checklist

Pro Tips

  • Mark your tent so you can easily find it at night.
  • Take a garbage bag to keep your campsite clean.
  • Set meeting points with your friends in case your phone dies.

Clothing

  • 4 Festival Outfits (pants, 1-shirt/crop tops, dresses/skirts)
  • Aussies (For the true gabbers)
  • 1-2 Jackets/Hoodies for in the evening
  • 1 Raincoat or poncho
  • Underwear (extra pair)
  • Socks (make sure to bring some extra pairs!)
  • Pajamas
  • Belt
  • Fanny pack (or other handbags you can take with you on the festival site)
  • Flip flops
  • Festival shoes + back-up shoes
  • Hat/cap (The sun can be a b*tch without them!)
  • Sunglasses (Not just for standing outside in the sun, might safe you from the security)
  • Swimsuit (sometimes useful if you don't like showering in the open)

Toiletries

  • Sunscreen + after sun
  • Deodorant (on some festivals, only rollers are allowed!)
  • Shower Gel + shampoo + conditioner
  • Face wipes
  • Cotton pads
  • Lip balm
  • Hand cream
  • Toothbrush + toothpaste
  • Make up + remover
  • Glasses and contact lens solution/case
  • feminine products
  • personal hygiene
  • hair dryer,styling iron, curler

Meds

  • Ibuprofen + other pain relief
  • Prescription meds (take your prescription with you!)
  • Nose spray
  • Allergy medication
  • Magnesium, 5HTP and other suplements
  • Band-aids, mini first-aid kit (There are many first aid employees and points on the festival side, make sure to go to them when needed)
  • anticonception

Camping

  • Tent
  • groundsheet
  • Pavilion
  • Sleeping bag
  • Sleeping pads/air mattresses incl pump
  • Pillow
  • Blanket
  • Flashlight
  • Camping chairs + table
  • Duct tape (you never know!)
  • Tie wraps
  • Padlock
  • Powerbanks
  • Earplugs (night) + eye mask
  • Games (beer pong)
  • Insect repellent
  • Wet wipes
  • Sanitizer
  • Towels

Food & Drinks

  • Water (!) + Empty bottles to fill
  • Soft drinks
  • Beer, wine, liquor + mixers
  • Chewing gum
  • Snacks
  • Cups and plates
  • Throw away bbq's with meat

Others

  • Music earplugs (!)
  • ID
  • Debit card, credit card (You don't necessarily need cash)
  • Phone + charger
  • Hand fan
  • Merchandise
  • Party dopper
  • Lighter/sigarets
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Instagram
  • Email
